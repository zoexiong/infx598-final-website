LGBTQ

"LGBTQ" is an abbreviation that originated in the 1990s and replaced what was formerly known as "the gay community." The abbreviation was created to be more inclusive of diverse groups. LGBTQ stands for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer (and/or questioning) individuals/identities. from The LGBTQ Community

On this site, we curated LGBTQ related information to let people learn more about this community.
